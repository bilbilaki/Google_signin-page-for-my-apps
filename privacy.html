Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 9/3/2025

This policy explains how the application AIBOX (its a fork of GPTBOX ) processes your information when you use Google Sign-In and other restricted Google functionalities. This app is designed for personal/limited use by close associates and is not intended for public distribution on stores; the sole purpose of this page is to comply with Google's verification requirements.

Information We Collect

  • Basic Google Account information: User ID, name, email, and profile picture (if available).
  • Google access/refresh tokens to enable app functionalities.
  • Minimal usage data (such as error logs) to improve performance and security.

How We Use Information

  • To authenticate and sign you into the app.
  • To provide optional features that you enable. For example (if used):
    • Sending emails on your behalf via Gmail (without reading your inbox).
    • Creating events or reminders in Google Calendar/Tasks.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
